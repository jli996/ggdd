**⚠️ 7 tags are 1-use** — they violate the taxonomy convention ("a tag should plausibly apply to 3+ guides"). Three options for each:
- (A) Accept the rarity (the concept is precise enough that future guides will use it)
- (B) Fold into a broader tag (e.g., `stamina-economy` → `economy`)
- (C) Drop the tag

My recommendation, per-tag:
- `tier-progression` (1) → **A (keep)**. Future v3 guides on MMO gear or crafting will use this.
- `stamina-economy` (1) → **A (keep)**. Specific mechanical signature; future guides on parry/super-meter systems will use it.
- `gc-free` (1) → **A (keep)**. Future perf guides will use it.
- `pool-reuse` (1) → **A (keep)**. Future perf guides will use it.
- `mmo` (1) → **C (drop)**. Folded into `persistent-world` + `coop` — those convey the same info.
- `telegraph-tells` (1) → **A (keep)**. Future action-game guides on enemy AI will use it.

If you accept the recommendations: taxonomy goes from 39 → 38 tags (dropping `mmo`). Otherwise reply with edits and I'll adjust.
